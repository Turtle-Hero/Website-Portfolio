About my Website

Nocturne Journal

Company: Augustana Art History Department

This site is responsive and was made for articles of Whistler's Criticism. I wanted to keep the feel of a book, while making the site more interactive with scroll arrows and a page count. The articles are still being written, so all of the text are placeholders, and the home page/table of contents is un-linked from the articles page until everything is ready.

Web Gallery

Company: Augustana Art History Department

This was my very first website! The goal for this one was to take a book and transform it into a website. It originally was an old auto-generated site. I replaced a lot of deprecated code, added essays to each item, and made the site responsive. I used JQuery to anchor the thumbnail image scroller to the bottom of the page, along with highlighting what page you are currently on.
